## Quick Answers

### Account & Authentication

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Visit app.yourdomain.com, click "Sign Up", enter your email and password, verify your email, and you're done!

**Q: What if I forgot my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, check your inbox for a reset link, and create a new password.

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**
A: No, each email can only have one account. Use different emails for different accounts if needed.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Q: Do you offer single sign-on (SSO)?**
A: Coming soon! Enterprise customers can request SSO integration now.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Why can't I log in?**
A: Common causes:
1. Wrong email/password - try "Forgot Password?"
2. Email not verified - check inbox for verification email
3. Account disabled - contact support@yourdomain.com
4. Browser cookies - clear cookies and try again

**Q: Why is the site so slow?**
A: Possible causes:
1. Slow internet connection - check your speed
2. Server load - try again in a few minutes
3. Browser cache - clear cache and refresh
4. Too many open tabs - close some and retry

**Q: The chat is stuck loading, what should I do?**
A: Try these in order:
1. Wait 30 seconds
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support

**Q: Why did my message fail to send?**
A: Likely causes:
1. No internet connection
2. Server error (usually temporary)
3. Out of tokens for the month
4. Session expired (refresh page)

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: No, you need internet to chat with AI. But you can view old conversations offline if they load first.

**Q: What are the system requirements?**
A: Modern browser, 1 Mbps internet, 50MB storage. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

**Q: Do you have a mobile app?**
A: Web app works on mobile. Native iOS/Android apps coming soon!

**Q: Is there a desktop app?**
A: Not yet. Web app works great on desktop. Desktop app on roadmap.
