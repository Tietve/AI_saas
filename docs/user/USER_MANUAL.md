## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Website**
   - Go to https://app.yourdomain.com

2. **Click "Sign Up"**
   - Or click "Create Account" link

3. **Enter Your Email**
   - Use your personal or work email
   - Make sure it's correct - we'll send you a verification link

4. **Create a Password**
   - At least 8 characters
   - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
   - Example: `MyPass@2024`

5. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the "Verify Email" link
   - Or copy the verification code and paste it in the app

6. **Start Chatting!**
   - You now have access to the Free plan
   - 10,000 tokens per month
   - Basic chat with GPT-3.5

### Your First Chat

1. **Click "New Conversation"**
   - Or click the "+" button

2. **Type Your Question**
   - Ask anything you like
   - "What is quantum computing?"
   - "Write a Python function for..."
   - "Explain machine learning"

3. **Press Enter or Click Send**
   - The AI will respond within a few seconds
   - You'll see the response appear below your message

4. **Continue the Conversation**
   - Type your next message
   - The AI remembers everything you said
   - It's like talking to someone familiar

## Account Management

### Signing In

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - https://app.yourdomain.com/login

2. **Enter Your Email & Password**
   - Use the email you signed up with
   - Password is case-sensitive

3. **Click "Sign In"**
   - You'll be taken to your dashboard

4. **Staying Logged In**
   - We'll keep you logged in for 24 hours
   - You can stay logged in on trusted devices
   - Close your browser to log out

**If You Remember Your Password:**
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter old password
4. Enter new password
5. Click "Update"

**If You Forgot Your Password:**
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link or copy the code
5. Enter your new password
6. Click "Reset Password"

### Updating Your Profile

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Click your avatar in top right
   - Click "Settings"

2. **Update Your Information**
   - Name: Your display name
   - Email: Your account email
   - Avatar: Upload a profile picture

3. **Save Changes**
   - Click "Save" button

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't share your data with anyone.

**Q: Can I export my conversations?**
A: Yes! Click the export icon on any conversation to download as PDF or Markdown.

**Q: Can I access from mobile?**
A: Yes! We have a responsive web app. Native apps coming soon.

**Q: Do you offer API access?**
A: Yes! Contact our sales team for enterprise API access.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Billing > Manage Subscription > Cancel. You'll stay on the Free plan.

**Q: Can I upgrade mid-month?**
A: Yes! You'll be charged the difference pro-rated for the remainder of the month.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for large accounts.

**Q: When does my billing cycle start?**
A: On the date you first subscribed. Your annual plan renews on the anniversary date.

**Q: Can I get an invoice?**
A: Yes! Go to Billing > Invoices > Download PDF.

### Chat Questions

**Q: Why did a conversation stop?**
A: Likely reasons:
- You ran out of tokens (check Usage)
- Lost internet connection
- Session timeout (24 hours)
- Server error (we'll investigate)

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: Very accurate for factual info, creative for writing. Always verify important facts.

**Q: Can I search my conversations?**
A: Yes! Use the search box in the sidebar. Searches across all your conversations.

**Q: What if the AI gives wrong information?**
A: It happens! The model can hallucinate. Always verify for important info.

**Q: Why is it slow sometimes?**
A: High demand, complex query, or selected model. Try GPT-3.5 for faster responses.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, use different emails. But we recommend keeping one account.

**Q: Can I share my account?**
A: No, that violates Terms of Service. Get a Family plan instead!

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: support@yourdomain.com or in-app chat widget.

**Q: Do you have a community forum?**
A: Yes! community.yourdomain.com - great tips and tricks there.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Error: "Invalid email or password"**
- Check email spelling
- Password is case-sensitive
- Try "Forgot Password?"

**Error: "Email not verified"**
- Check your inbox for verification email
- Click verification link
- Or use resend verification

**Error: "Account disabled"**
- Contact support
- Usually because of payment issues
- We can reactivate once issues resolved

### Chat Not Working

**Message stuck sending**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page
- Try again

**AI not responding**
- Check if you have tokens left
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different model

**Error message appears**
- Note the error code
- Screenshot it
- Contact support with details

### Payment Issues

**Card declined**
- Check expiration date
- Check enough funds
- Try different card

**Can't update card**
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cookies
- Contact support

**Duplicate charge**
- Contact support
- We'll investigate within 24 hours
- We issue refunds for errors
